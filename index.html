Mutual-Aid Organizations for Latinx Community

By Asmy Fayad

There are a lot of organizations helping the Latinx Community in Bushwick get non-exploitative legal advice, provide them food and health services but a lot of the time people are not aware of the existence of these organizations. Therefore, you will be able to find the support you need by looking at the icons to go to the right place.

List of Services

Non-exploitative Legal Advice



The organization of Laundry Workers Center and El Hormiguero support immigrant workers that are being exploited by their employers. Both of these organizations hold protests for the workers to raise their voices and demand fair payment and sanitary conditions while doing their duty.

Health Services

The organization of Make the Road and Mexican Coalition provide health services. These organizations help immigrants find a good health insurance and also inform them about how to take care of their nutrition, how to prevent illnesses plus other things.

Food and Pantry Items



Bushwick Ayuda Mutua is an organization that provides food or pantry items to all families in the district of Bushwick. Although they don't provide meals they give fruits, vegetables, beans, rice, and other food items for people who do not have enough money to afford the essentials to live.
